About Me

My name is Vuk Radovic and I come from the capital of Serbia – Belgrade. Entering year 8 of elementary school, I decided to fully commit myself to studying Maths, Physics and related fields. Ten years later, I hold a Masters in Mathematical and Theoretical Physics from Oxford and work as a quantitative researcher. However, as I delved deeper into my primary occupation, I realized that my connection to my other passions was gradually weakening. This blog serves as a platform to maintain consistency, focus, and enhance the quality of my pursuits that may not have direct, measurable outcomes.

Embarking on a Quest for Virtue

We live in an era where the influx of information is unparalleled. Our brains are constantly bombarded with dopamine hits from social media, video games, alcohol, drugs, and other addictive behaviors. In chasing these fleeting pleasures, we often sacrifice long-term rewards that come from hard work and skill acquisition. Mere awareness of this issue is insufficient to reclaim our path towards eudemonia, the Ancient Greek concept of genuine, lasting happiness. This blog chronicles my journey to pursue virtue and rediscover a sense of purpose and fulfillment.

Maintaining Focus through Reflection

Writing and explaining concepts can lead to a deeper understanding of the subject matter. The process of organizing and articulating our thoughts often solidifies our knowledge. By regularly writing about intriguing ideas, I aim to not only improve my consistency but also gain a more profound comprehension of the topics I explore.

Topics

Though my primary focus will be on Maths, Physics, Machine Learning, and other technical fields, I will also venture into areas that pique my interest, despite my limited proficiency. These eclectic explorations may cover Philosophy, Music, Writing, and Language Learning, among others. Check out my posts to see how I rediscover my passions, learn from my experiences, and strive for personal growth.
